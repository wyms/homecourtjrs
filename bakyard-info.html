Tournament Basics

✅ What to Bring

  • 🧊 Cooler (welcome!)
  • 💺 Chair (welcome!)
  • 🧴 Sunscreen
  • 🕶️ Sunglasses
  • 🏖️ Towels

❌ What NOT to Bring

  • 🍺 Glass bottles
  • 🐕 Dogs (not allowed)
  • 👶 Unsupervised children

🚗 PARKING INSTRUCTIONS - VERY IMPORTANT

❌ Do NOT drive in front of the church building

Follow these steps:

  1. Enter from South Entrance
  2. Follow road around to the temporary buildings
  3. Look for an orange cone
  4. Take the dirt pathway all the way to the back lot
  5. You'll see a red and blue barn
  6. Walk through the back gate
  7. Stay to the LEFT on the cement pathway
  8. ❌ DO NOT GO RIGHT
  9. Courts are at the end of the cement path

🏡 Bakyard Etiquette

Please remember this is our home — we love dogs, but no dogs are allowed in the backyard.

⚠️

Supervise children closely. There are hazards on the property that could be dangerous.

🤸

Only one person at a time is allowed on the trampoline.

🤝

We're happy to welcome everyone here, but please be respectful of the space and others.

Thank you! — The Backyard
