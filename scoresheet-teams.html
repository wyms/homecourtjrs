Quick Reference - King of the Court Rules

  • Win on Challenger's side = move to King
  • Lose on King's side = go to Challenger
  • Only King's side can score points
  • 15 minutes per round
  • 1-minute timeout after 10 minutes
  • Top 5 teams advance to final
  • Maximum 8 seconds between rallies
