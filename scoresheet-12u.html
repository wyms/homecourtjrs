8-Team Tournament Format

Rounds 1-3:

Round 1: Teams 1,2,3,4,5 (Teams 6,7,8 sit)

Round 2: Teams 1,2,3,6,7 (Teams 4,5,8 sit)

Round 3: Teams 1,4,5,6,8 (Teams 2,3,7 sit)

Rounds 4-6:

Round 4: Teams 2,4,7,8,3 (Teams 1,5,6 sit)

Round 5: Teams 3,5,6,7,2 (Teams 1,4,8 sit)

Round 6: Teams 4,5,8,1,7 (Teams 2,3,6 sit)

Each team plays exactly 5 rounds and sits 1 round

Scoring Notes:

  • Enter points earned per round
  • Calculate total points across all rounds
  • Rank teams by total points (highest = 1st)
  • Use head-to-head for tiebreakers

Championship Advancement:

  • Top 5 teams advance to final round
  • Final round determines tournament winner
  • Remaining teams ranked by qualifying points
