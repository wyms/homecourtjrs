🥈 Semifinal Round (Round 7)

📊 Top 10 Teams (by cumulative points) advance

Court A: Teams ranked 1st, 3rd, 5th, 7th, 9th (5 teams)
Court B: Teams ranked 2nd, 4th, 6th, 8th, 10th (5 teams)
🎯 Top 5 overall from this round advance to finals!

🏆 Final Round (Round 8)

🌟 Top 5 Teams (from semifinal) compete

Single Court: All 5 teams play King of the Court format
Duration: 10-12 minutes
🥇 Highest scoring team wins the tournament!

Summary by Team:

Teams 1-10: Each plays 3 rounds, sits 3 rounds
Teams 11-20: Each plays 3 rounds, sits 3 rounds
Top 10: Advance to semifinal round 7
Top 5: Advance to final round 8

Alternating Format! Teams alternate between playing (3 rounds) and sitting (3 rounds) during the regular tournament. This keeps everyone engaged while allowing for strategic rest periods.

Requires: Two courts running simultaneously with 5-team King of the Court format. Each round is 10-12 minutes long decided by the tournament director. Total tournament time: 6 regular rounds + 2 championship rounds.
